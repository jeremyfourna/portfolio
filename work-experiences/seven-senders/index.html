Features & Projects I worked on

Launch of Sendwise

In 6 months we launched Sendwise, the new version of the previous product called Analytics. Analytics was a pure PHP application, with Sendwise we introduced a React front-end on top of the Analytics back-end.
Sendwise allow the creation of an unlimited amount of tracking pages that you can customize to your needs. You can also customize all emails send to the customers via the application.

Carrier messages notifications

Each carrier has its own set of messages to say the same thing (Package scanned at the hub, Package delivered, Delivery attempt failed, etc.). We improved our internal mapping to allow more use cases for our notifications. For each notification, the brand could decide to display it on their tracking pages, send an email to the customer or to their internal users.

Integration of Looker

Looker is a Business Intelligence (BI) tool, that we integrated inside Sendwise to allow our customers to build custom dashboards about their delivery data.

Carrier comparison

Allow a brand to analize which carrier is the best to deliver their products via various KPI and filters.

Heat map to display delivery time from a warehouse

Visualize on a map the delivery time from a warehouse to any region in Europe. Brands can see via a color coding quickly in which regions, they are not performing well. This map was done with Leaflet and GADM data sets.

About Basket-Ball 🏀
I have started 3 projects along the years that touch each an area of Basket-Ball.
Basket Ball live stats

I played Basket-Ball between 8 and 18 years old, at youth high level in France. Taking the game statistics was left to the assistant coach and parents that knew how to take the. You needed to wait for the end of the game to know how well you played or how many turnovers your teams did in the game.

I build a small app that allow anyone to take the statistics of a Basket-Ball game and share them in real time with the world. That way you do not need to wait for the end of the game to know how well your child did in the game or if your favorite local team is doing in their game when they play outside.

💻 Github repository

Basket Ball strategy

When a coach shared with us the game strategies and plays we will play in game, she/he used paper or whiteboard with step by step instructions and later real life repetitions on the court. You could only learn and train them during the practices.

I wanted to build a tool for coach and players to share the strategies and plays, be able to see them in motion to understand faster how the play will be played.
